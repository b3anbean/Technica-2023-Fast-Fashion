Sustainability Facts

Materials:
  • Recycled Polyester
  • Recycled Wool
  • Lyocell
  • Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA)
Lyocell Sustainability Facts:

According to WWF, it takes more than 20,000 liters (5,283 gallons) of water to produce just one kilogram (2.2 pounds) of cotton, which roughly equals one T-shirt and a pair of jeans.

Polyester Sustainability Facts:

According to the carbon offset company 8 Billion Trees, "producing 2 square meters of polyester creates 6.4kg carbon emissions."
