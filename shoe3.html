Hoka

Clifton 9

Hoka Clifton 9s are great for running laps around Eppley or embarking on a long trip to class. They’re easily the best contender for walking up STAMP Hill and pair well with athletic gear or a casual look.

Sustainability Facts

Materials:

70% Recycled Nylon
30% Recycled Polyester

Nylon Sustainability Facts:

The manufacturing process of the nylon fabric is energy and water-intensive. On average, producing 1kg of nylon consumes 138.62 MJ and 663kg of water.

Polyester Sustainability Facts:

Two square meters of polyester is equal to 6.4kg carbon emissions, 32.5km distance driven, and 164.9m3 of CO2 gas.
